Axel Bartsch Soccer Profile

My name is Axel Bartsch and I am a United States Virgin Islands international footballer. I am 18 years old (Born May 16, 2002) and I am a midfielder or defender. I currently attend Occidental College and will be playing my first SCIAC season in the fall. In highschool I played for Virgin Islands Montessori School and Peter Gruber International Academy.
